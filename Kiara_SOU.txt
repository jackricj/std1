Simple Columnar Epithelium

Microvilli are tiny finger-like projections on the surface of cells that increase the surface area for absorption and secretion. Simple columnar epithelium with microvilli is commonly found in the lining of the small intestine, where the microvilli greatly enhance the absorption of nutrients from digested food into the bloodstream. Their presence increases the efficiency of nutrient absorption by maximizing the available surface area for absorption.
